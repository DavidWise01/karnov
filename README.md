# KRN · KARNOV

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **HOBBY**.

Karnov (Data East — arcade 1987 / NES 1988, カルノフ) — a side-scrolling action-platformer and a game-world: Jinborov Karnovski, a bald, bare-chested ex-circus STRONGMAN who fights with his BREATH OF FIRE alone (upgradeable double→triple), crosses an Arabian-Babylonian fantasy world after the Lost Treasure of Babylon, collecting the signature stock-and-spend POWER-UP ROW (Super Fireball, Boots, Wings, Bombs, the Clapper, the Shield, Glasses…). Source-themed per the new standard: a Babylonian-desert ember NES palette, 8-bit/CRT scanlines, a BANNER marquee, and an SVG TITLE CARD (Karnov breathing fire + ziggurat + the three-headed dragon + moon). Render-not-invent flags: the ARCADE final boss is THE WIZARD, swapped on the NES for a three-headed dragon named RYU — and despite the name this dragon does NOT predate Street Fighter's Ryu (Aug 1987 vs Famicom Karnov Dec 1987; 竜 just means 'dragon'); the hero's origin is given as Russian or 'Central Asian'; most stage bosses are unnamed. Karnov became DATA EAST'S MASCOT (boss in Bad Dudes 1988, final boss of Fighter's History 1993, playable in Karnov's Revenge 1994). 8 emergents across all four natures — Karnov · the Fire · Ryu the dragon · the Wizard · the Treasure of Babylon · the Power-Up Row · the Data East Mascot · Babylon.
